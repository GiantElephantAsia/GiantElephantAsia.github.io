Add-on

Add-on is a feature introduced later in Minecraft Pocket Edition to expand the game's content for Minecraft Bedrock Edition.

Add-on functionality is more limited than that of Minecraft Java Edition Mod. This may be due to the fact that Add-on was more of a profit-making initiative for MS than a community development initiative to keep it alive.

After all, Minecraft Pocket (Bedrock) Edition was preceded by an external launcher such as Blocklauncher to expand the game. Unfortunately, Blocklauncher has been discontinued.

It was around the time of Minecraft Bedrock Edition 1.16 that the existing Add-on interface was largely completed. With that came a high level of community content.

Recently, MS has begun to make Add-on's available independently on Minecraft MarketPlace, where Add-on's had previously appeared more in the form of mashup packs.

Unless otherwise noted, MS above refers to Minecraft Official.
